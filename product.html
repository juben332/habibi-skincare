Application Guide

  1. 1

    Cleanse your skin

    Start with our Luminous Gel Cleanser on dry or damp skin. Rinse thoroughly and pat dry gently.

  2. 2

    Tone

    Apply Rose Water Balancing Toner with a cotton pad to balance pH and maximise serum absorption.

  3. 3

    Apply the serum

    Dispense 3–4 drops onto fingertips. Press gently into skin from centre outwards. Use AM and PM.

  4. 4

    Moisturise & protect

    Follow with Desert Rose Moisturizer and finish with Veil SPF 50+ in the morning.

Pro tip: For enhanced results, apply to slightly damp skin. The moisture helps drive the actives deeper for maximum efficacy.
