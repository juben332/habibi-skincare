Our Story

Where Luxury Meets
Pure Nature

Habibi Skincare was born from a simple yet profound belief: that true beauty is nurtured, not manufactured. Founded in the heart of Davao City, we blend the lush botanicals of the Philippine tropics with time-honoured Middle Eastern beauty rituals.

Every product we create is a love letter to your skin — formulated with the purest ingredients, free from harmful chemicals, and crafted with the kind of care that only comes from deep devotion.

"Habibi" — an Arabic word meaning "my love." Because every ritual should feel like an act of love for yourself.
