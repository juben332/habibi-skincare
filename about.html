How It Began

The Origin Story

Habibi Skincare began as a personal obsession. Our founder, raised in Davao City with deep roots in Middle Eastern heritage, spent years searching for skincare that honoured both the lush tropical botanicals of the Philippines and the luxurious beauty rituals of the Arab world.

What started as handcrafted batches shared with friends and family quickly grew into something far greater — a brand built on the conviction that the finest natural ingredients, handled with care and intention, can transform not just your skin, but your relationship with yourself.

"Habibi" — Arabic for "my love" — isn't just our name. It's the promise embedded in every product we make. That every ritual should feel like an act of devotion to yourself.

"I wanted every woman to experience what it feels like to truly care for her skin — not as a chore, but as a ceremony."

— Founder, Habibi Skincare

What We Stand For

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make luxury natural skincare accessible to every Filipino — crafted with the finest botanicals the world offers, and the care that only deep devotion can provide. We exist to make you feel beautiful in your most natural state.

Our Vision

To become Southeast Asia's most trusted luxury natural skincare brand — proving that purity and prestige are not opposites, but partners. A future where every skincare ritual is a moment of conscious self-care, rooted in respect for the earth.

Our Principles

What We Believe In

These are not just values on a wall — they are the decisions we make every day.

Purity First

Every ingredient has a reason to exist. If it doesn't serve your skin, it doesn't go in the bottle. No fillers, no fluff.

Made with Love

"Habibi" means "my love". Every product is formulated, tested, and crafted with genuine care — for you and for your skin.

Locally Rooted

Proudly made in Davao City. We source local botanicals, support Filipino suppliers, and celebrate Philippine biodiversity.

Planet Responsible

Our packaging is recyclable, our supply chain is transparent, and our commitment to the earth is unwavering.

Ingredient Philosophy

Only the Rarest, the Purest

We travel the world — from the saffron fields of Iran to the argan groves of Morocco, the rose valleys of Bulgaria, and the rich tropical forests of Mindanao — to source the finest natural actives available.

Every ingredient is ethically sourced, sustainably harvested, and rigorously tested before it meets your skin. We are transparent about what we use and why — because we believe you deserve to know exactly what you are putting on your face.

We are certified cruelty-free, free from over 2,000 harmful chemicals, and committed to continuous improvement in both formula and practice.
